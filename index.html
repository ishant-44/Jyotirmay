Message from the Founder

I have spent forty years working closely with the development of children and adults with disabilities. Jyotirmay Centre was founded in February 2019 to create a space where individuals can learn, grow, and build meaningful lives. Our centre supports adults aged 18 to 50, many from underprivileged backgrounds, by providing emotional, social, and skill-based development.

About Jyotirmay

Jyotirmay is a Skills Training, Therapeutic and Recreational Centre for adults with disabilities. We focus on improving quality of life through functional, physical, and vocational training, helping individuals become confident and independent members of society.

Our Mission

To help persons with disabilities learn, grow, and feel empowered to achieve their dreams and become equal participants in society.

Our Vision

To create a life without limits for persons with disabilities by improving their quality of life through training and support.

Our Objectives

  • Develop self-reliance and life skills
  • Provide vocational training and employment opportunities
  • Offer therapy to improve functional abilities
  • Encourage independent living skills
  • Promote confidence through community participation
  • Support and guide parents and families

Our Services

Vocational Skills

Block printing, candle making, pottery, paper bag making, jewellery making, cooking, computers.

Therapy

Physiotherapy to improve movement, functionality, and overall well-being.

Co-curricular Activities

Yoga, dance, sports, music, and drama.

Independent Living Skills

Training in hygiene, nutrition, travel, and personal safety.

Placement

Job training in hotels, offices, data entry, and housekeeping.

Utilization of Funds

Donations are used for raw materials, student stipends, and operational expenses. Sponsorship helps support students from low-income backgrounds.

Cost per student: ₹5000/month
