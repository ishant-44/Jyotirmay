About Jyotirmay

Jyotirmay is a Skills Training Therapeutic and Recreational Centre for adults with disability. Our vision is to create a life without limits for persons with disabilities. Towards this, we want to improve their quality of life with functional, physical and vocational training. Our mission is to help persons with disabilities to learn, endure and feel empowered to venture out further and realize their dreams, thus creating a place for themselves as equals in a diverse society. We were very fortunate that Namrata Kanwar, who had volunteered earlier with this group, was associated with her family trust and very generously offered us their premises to house the centre. She and her brother Parag Vora have proved invaluable allies all through. Their Charitable Trust provides Jyotirmay an umbrella to help us settle down in our first few years. Their help and support have enabled us to grow and we are now an established centre with 40 Bonafide students and are now looking for ways to sustain the organization and expand our services for the students. Our Objectives We plan to achieve our mission through: • Making students/trainees self-reliant, helping them to develop various life skills and vocational training. • Finding placement for those who can go into open employment. • Providing therapy to increase functional abilities. • Providing an environment to enhance independent living skills. • Participating in community events to in still and enhance self-esteem & confidence in the person with disability. • Providing support and guidance to the parents and students/trainees.

Our Mission

To help persons with disabilities learn, grow, and feel empowered to achieve their dreams and become equal participants in society.

Our Vision

To create a life without limits for persons with disabilities by improving their quality of life through training and support.
