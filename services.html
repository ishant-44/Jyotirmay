Jyotirmay

Our Objectives

  • Develop self-reliance and life skills
  • Provide vocational training and employment opportunities
  • Offer therapy to improve functional abilities
  • Encourage independent living skills
  • Promote confidence through community participation
  • Support and guide parents and families

Our Services

Vocational Skills

Block printing, candle making, pottery, paper bag making, jewellery making, cooking, computers and functional literacy.

Therapy

: Physiotherapy helps focus on function movement and optimal use of the persons potential. It uses physical approaches to maintain and restore physical, psychological and social well-being within all environments of the person including home, school, recreation and community environments.

Co-curricular Activities

: provide for socialization. These are an essential part of life and help in enhancing the learning process. Co-curricular activities bring social and intellectual skills, moral values and character appeal in students. The different cocurricular activities provided are yoga, dance, sports, music and drama.

Independent Living Skills

Training is provided to develop and enhance the ability to take care of themselves, their own needs of hygiene and nutrition, travelling and security.

Placement

Skills are taught which prepare them for becoming active members in society to earn a livelihood. They are sent for on the job training to hotels and restaurants for housekeeping and kitchen skill training, to offices for data entry, and to train as peons and telephone operators.
